Services

Technical soil science for practical decisions

Services can be tailored to individual sites, research programs, consulting teams, public agencies, universities, and land managers.

Soil Evaluation and Characterization

Field-based soil investigation to evaluate soil morphology, variability, and limiting conditions affecting land use and development.

Services include

  • Soil description, morphology, and characteristics
  • Identification of restrictive layers and hydric soils
  • Soil classification and interpretation for land-use decisions
  • Soil documentation and technical reporting
  • Soil evaluation for onsite wastewater treatment in Nebraska

Soil Data, GIS and Geospatial Analysis

Spatial analysis using modern geospatial tools to evaluate soil, environmental, and landscape data.

Services include

  • GIS-based soil and environmental data analysis
  • Integration of soil observations with climate, geology, and land-cover datasets
  • Spatial modeling and predictive soil mapping
  • Map production and geospatial visualization
  • Support for research and land-management projects

Soil Science Education and Instruction

Professional education in the fundamentals of soil science for state agencies, universities, consultants, and land managers.

Topics include

  • Soil genesis and landscape diversity
  • Soil morphology and field description
  • Soil classification and interpretation
  • Field methods in soil investigation and mapping
  • Application of soil science to land management

Instruction is available through workshops, field demonstrations, and guest lectures.

About the principal

Rigorous field science, clear interpretation

Auger & Spade is led by Ryan Hodges, Ph.D., a soil scientist with extensive experience in soil classification, soil morphology, and soil-landscape interpretation.

Ryan combines field- and data-based soil investigation with modern geospatial analysis to better understand soil variability across landscapes. His work applies fundamental soil science to practical questions involving land use, management, development, and research.

Auger & Spade was established to provide technically rigorous, science-based soil characterization services with findings communicated in a clear and useful way.

Based in Lincoln, Nebraska
Primary focus Soil evaluation, GIS, and education
